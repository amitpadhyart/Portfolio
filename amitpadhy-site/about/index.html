About Me

I'm Amit Padhy, a final-year B.Tech student at A.C. Patil College of Engineering in Mumbai, graduating in 2026. I build full-stack web applications — the kind that actually get deployed, not just demoed on localhost.

My stack centres around the MERN ecosystem: MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. I care about clean MVC architecture, async error handling, and making sure data doesn't escape without proper validation. Shipping without tests gives me anxiety.

Beyond writing code, I've led technical workshops for 50+ students at my college and competed in 3+ intercollegiate hackathons — events that taught me more about pressure-testing ideas than any classroom ever did.

I'm currently exploring VoxScript, a scripting and document automation project I believe can take some of what LaTeX does and make it dramatically more approachable. Follow along on GitHub.
